# EDA Findings

## Market Reach

Steam success follows a long-tail distribution.
Most games achieve limited reach, while a small
percentage become high impact titles.

## Reception

Games with fewer than 20 reviews were removed
because review percentages were unstable.

## Pricing

Higher market reach games tend to have slightly
higher price points, suggesting pricing contains
useful market information.

## Platforms

Multi-platform releases show higher average reach.

## Release Trends

Steam releases increased rapidly after 2015.
Game age must be considered because older games
have had more time to accumulate owners.
